Introduction To Using Lean

In this section, you'll acquire the skills to craft proofs in Lean. Initially, we'll delve into a mechanistic approach, providing you with the ability to express any natural deduction proof in Lean.

Subsequently, we'll explore certain features within Lean that facilitate a more forward-directed approach to argumentation. These mechanisms allow for proofs that resemble informal reasoning but can seamlessly transition into natural deduction, providing insight into the correlation between the two styles of proof.
